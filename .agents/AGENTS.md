# Techies Community Website — Project Rules

## Design & Color Theme Strictness
- **Strict Theme Colors Only**: Do NOT add any accent colors or palettes (such as green, emerald, teal, neon, or bright unaligned hues) outside our official website theme (`#030509` background, dark slate panels `#060a12`, crisp white/slate text, and subtle aurora/space gradients).
- **No Neon**: Never use neon colors anywhere on the website (`#00FFFF`, `#FF00FF`, bright neon green/yellow, etc.).
- **Consistency Across All CTAs**: Even when adding external or third-party CTAs (like WhatsApp, Discord, or Instagram buttons), maintain our official aurora/space theme styling (`bg-gradient-to-b from-white via-slate-100 to-slate-200 text-neutral-950` or `border border-white/20 bg-white/[0.05] text-white`). Do NOT introduce brand colors (like WhatsApp emerald/green) that clash with the site's dark space aesthetic.
